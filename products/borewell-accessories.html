Accessory FAQs

Which rope material is better â€“ Nylon or SS?

Nylon is economical and sufficient for smaller pumps. Stainless Steel is recommended for long-term installations and heavier pumps.

Do I need a panel board if I already have a starter?

Yes, panel boards provide advanced protections and monitoring, especially for higher HP pumps.

How to choose cable length and size?

Cable length depends on the depth of bore and installation distance. Use copper cables with adequate thickness for voltage drop control.
